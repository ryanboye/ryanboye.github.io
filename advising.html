Advisory Services

Finding product market fit is hard. I can help you simplify the process. I have over a decade of experience helping small startups find their footing. Let me help yours. I offer a variety of packages depending on your stage.

Free 1hr consultation and free advice for anybody interested. hello@ryanboye.com

Package Tiers

Tier 1 – 
Basic Advisory

A simple monthly retainer for a set number of hours of advisory services. It's suitable for startups or businesses that need occasional guidance on their product design and strategy.

Services:
• Email support
• monthly strategy calls (e.g., 2-4 hours total per month)
• high-level product reviews.


Cost: Fixed monthly fee, lower than other tiers due to the limited engagement.

Tier 2 —
Strategic Advisory

A more involved engagement, regular check ins and guidance. This tier is for companies requiring deeper insights into product-market fit and UX design strategies.

Services: Includes everything in Tier 1 plus:
• Bi-weekly calls
• Product design critiques
• Detailed reports on product improvements
• Customer experience optimization strategies
• Assistance in customer research analysis.

Cost: A higher fixed monthly fee reflecting the increased time and value, or a mix of fee plus equity for long-term engagements.

Tier 3 –
Comprehensive Advisory and Execution Planning

The highest level of engagement, designed for companies that need extensive, ongoing support in executing their product strategy. I will get involved directly.

Services: All services in Tiers 2, plus:
• Weekly check-ins
• Hands-on work with the design and product teams
• Guidance on implementing changes
• Regular customer feedback analysis.

This tier might also include workshops or training sessions for company staff on design thinking and customer-centric product development

Cost: A premium monthly fee or a negotiated mix of fee and equity, recognizing the substantial commitment and direct impact on the company's product strategy and market success.
